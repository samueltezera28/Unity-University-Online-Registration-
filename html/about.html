About Unity University

Unity University is a private university in Ethiopia.
It is the first privately owned institute of higher learning to be awarded full-fledged university
status in Ethiopia by the Ministry of Education. It is also the first private university in the country
to offer postgraduate programs.Established in 1991. In March 1998 the institute was upgraded to a college level, following its achievements in education, becoming the first privately owned college in Ethiopia.
